About

"It's The Game of Life." was a game created by some of the greatest students at Google CSSI Chicago 2015. We created this game as a final project in our program that showcases a lot of the skills we learned in less than three weeks! We wanted to create a game that was creative, hilarious, and unpredictable. Here's more about the people who created the game:
